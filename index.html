About Our Heckin Good Business

Heckin' Good Bubble Tea is owned and operated by Bryan and Amanda Nishi. It will open in the summer of 2021. After years of working for other companies the we decided it was time to work for ourselves. We first landed on opening a poutine truck name Poutine on the Ritz, but that idea never came to fruition. Finally deciding on a Boba Tea truck thanks to our lame sense of humor and love of delicious bubble tea!
